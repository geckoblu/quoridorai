Game rules

Quoridor is a turn-based game for two players.

At your turn, you may either move your pawn or place a wall, if you have any walls left.

If your pawn reaches the other side of the board before your opponent's does, you win.

If your pawn is adjacent to your opponent's, you may jump over it, either straight or at a right angle.

You are not allowed to place a wall if it would block either player from reaching their goal.

 

 

How to use

Simply click on the board to move your pawn or place a wall.
